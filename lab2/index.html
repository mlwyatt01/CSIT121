Sweet Potato Chili - The Best Comfort and Healthy Soup Ever

If you like chili - then give this recipe a try. It's the soup you didnt know that you needed in your life. Its filling, loaded with protein, stores easy in the fridge for a few days and even keeps in the freezer for future meal preps.

Ingedients Needed

Grab paper and pen because the ingedients for this soup and get real lengthy:

  • Ground Turkey
  • Sweet Potatoes
  • Chicken Broth
  • Quinoa
  • Frozen Corn
  • Frozen Holy Trinity
  • Navy Beans
  • Chili Seasoning packet
  • lots of herbs and spices

Lets get to cooking

  1. In a large pot over medium heat, add oil. Add the clean and peeled sweet potatoes and cook until soft, about 15 minutes.
  2. Then add ground turkey with the chili seasoning and continuing cooking until the turkey is cooked.
  3. Then add the remaining ingredients, bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover with lid, and let simmer for another 15-20 minutes.
  4. Season to taste.
